Data Analyst

Job Description

- Develop full-funnel performance marketing and marketing optimization strategies, through close collaboration with marketing teams and relevant parties, both internal and external, supported with comprehensive measurement and diagnostic research of broad and targeted campaigns.

- Evaluate and analyze the effectiveness of marketing programs, across different campaigns, segments, mechanics, channels, and communications.

- Work with data science on Marketing Mix Modeling to derive omnichannel marketing attribution which is not apparent from looking at charts and tables, and partner with relevant parties to set up tests based on the modeling.

- Assist in the setup of solid testing methodologies for promotions and advertising campaigns to determine the impact on both top and bottom-line results.

- Design and standardize dashboards for marketing to track performance along the marketing funnel.

Qualification

- 3 years in a business analytics role, with strong business acumen, collaborative leadership, and sound data storytelling and visualization skills.

- Experience setting up complex tests which identify insights beyond the normal.

- Basic statistical skills and understanding.

- Previous retail and ecommerce experience a plus.

- SQL, Python, or R programming skills are plus.
